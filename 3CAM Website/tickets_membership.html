  • Membership Information



    By becoming a member, your next visit is free (membership holder only), and are entitled to discounted ticket rates for future visits. You are also eligible for exclusive event invites free of charge. Membership will be automatically renewed annually unless otherwise requested.


    3CAM is committed to the environment. Aside from receiving your Membership ID in the mail, all member communications are paperless and will be sent via email or text.


    Subscribe to the 3CAM mailing list to be the first to receive exclusive offers, news, and updates. You may opt out at any time.

    You may also enter your email address at the bottom of the page to join the mailing list.
